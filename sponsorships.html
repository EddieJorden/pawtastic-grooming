Sponsorships

At Pamela's Pawtastic Grooming, we believe in giving back to the community and supporting important causes. We are proud to sponsor various events and organizations throughout the year. Check out our current sponsorship below:

AIDS Life Cycle Ride

Pamela's Pawtastic Grooming is a proud sponsor of the AIDS Life Cycle Ride, a 545-mile bike ride from San Francisco to Los Angeles that raises funds and awareness for the HIV/AIDS services provided by the Los Angeles LGBT Center and the San Francisco AIDS Foundation.

Event Details:

  • Date: June 4th - June 10th
  • Route: San Francisco to Los Angeles
  • Distance: 545 miles

To donate:

Visit the Sponsor Pamela's Ride to make a donation to our team.

How to Get Involved:

If you would like to participate in the ride or support this important cause, visit the AIDS Life Cycle website for more information on how to register, donate, or volunteer.
