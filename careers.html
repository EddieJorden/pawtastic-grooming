Junior Pet Groomer (New Graduates)

We are looking for recent graduates from dog grooming schools to join our team as Junior Pet Groomers. This is a great opportunity for individuals who are passionate about pets and have a strong desire to learn and grow in the grooming industry.

  • Recent graduate from a certified dog grooming school
  • Ability to handle pets with care and patience
  • Strong communication and customer service skills
  • Team player with a positive attitude

To apply, please send your resume, cover letter, and a copy of your grooming school certificate to TangDolan@aol.com with the subject line "Junior Pet Groomer Application".

Pet Grooming Internship (New Graduates)

Our Pet Grooming Internship is designed for new graduates who want to gain hands-on experience in a professional grooming environment. As an intern, you will work closely with our experienced groomers and learn valuable skills to jumpstart your grooming career.

  • Recent graduate from a certified dog grooming school
  • Eager to learn and grow in the grooming industry
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills

To apply, please send your resume, cover letter, and a copy of your grooming school certificate to TangDolan@aol.com with the subject line "Pet Grooming Internship Application".
